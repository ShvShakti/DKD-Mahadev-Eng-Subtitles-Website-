Personal testimony — Why this exists

This project grew from deep personal change. Mahadev's katha has changed my life — brought discipline, peace and purpose. Many foreign viewers on Star Bharat's YouTube repeatedly asked to understand the story; I made English subtitles to share the healing and guidance of these tales. I operate this alone, sincerely, without any commercial motive — my wish is that these teachings help others as they did me.
